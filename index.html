Octolert monitors your Axosoft Glo Boards and alerts you by playing sounds and flashing the lights with any changes you want to keep track of. Totally cross-platform; you will even be able to hook it up to a raspberry pi with some attached speakers to have your own ‘Octobuddy’ let you know what’s going on.

Start it running with just one simple command and then integrate it with Glo Boards using a super simple web console; accessible from anywhere on your network. Sign up to our mailing list to be the first to know when this fun productivity tool is released.
